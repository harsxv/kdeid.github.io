GO-Indonesia is an event held by GO-JEK Indonesia team, GO-Indonesia is a forum to inspire creative industry and social entrepreneur activist in Indonesia to share their knowledge and their thoughts to create a better Indonesia. This event show ideas and share experiences to inspire youth to take action to contribute to their nation.

In this project, I was developed the landing page with my colleague and I was in charge developing HTML and CSS to implement the design.
