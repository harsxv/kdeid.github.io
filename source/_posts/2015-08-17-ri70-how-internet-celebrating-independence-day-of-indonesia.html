How the world sees Indonesia? Indonesia become famous in internet in 2010 to 2015 I suppose, Indonesia become second largest facebook user, large user of twitter, and large of internet market which is have massive growth on it. I love how internet user sees Indonesia, people see us as massive army in the Internet, some people hate us, some people laugh, some people love us.

Actually, I love the internet population sees us as Indonesia, that polandball above is actually cute. In the other side, Indonesia is a big market for internet company, even Facebook, Twitter and Google praise us, giving us special treat in every occasion. So, how the big three internet company make us special?

Google

Google have a special doodle on their search engine, Indonesian flag on GOOGLE and "Tugu Proklamasi" as OO letter. I love the details even the artist put tropical plant in the background, as you can see coconut trees on the doodle.

[caption id="attachment_3975" align="alignnone" width="500"] Google Doodle toay.[/caption]

Facebook

This largest social media platform that Indonesian use have their way to celebrate our independence day, if you logged in for first time today, you'll asked "Are you celebrating Indonesian Independence Day today?" and it will put " celebrating Indonesian Independence Day." thing on your status. Quite good, isn't it?

[caption id="attachment_3976" align="alignnone" width="524"] Facebook way to celebrate our independence day.[/caption]

Twitter

Try to post anything with #RI70 hashtag and twitter will give you a little badge on your tweet. I don't have much word to say but this little thing so meaningful.
